Project goal: fast glanceable maritime navigation and dispatch UX for small vessel and fishing boat operators; emphasize safety, real‑time telemetry, and one‑tap actions.

Tone: modern, friendly, maritime‑technical, trustworthy.

Brand constraints: distinct identity; do not reuse ZUS logos, imagery, or exact color codes. Treat ZUS as a compositional and interaction reference only.

Visual system

Primary colors: Teal #0F9AA7; Deep Navy #082837.

Accent colors: Coral #FF6B61; Sand #F6EBD9.

Neutrals: Off‑white #FBFAF8; Muted Gray #6B7785.

Typography: Geometric sans (Inter or Poppins); weights: Bold for headings, Medium for CTAs, Regular for body. Suggested sizes: 28/24/20 for headings, 14–16 for body, 12 for helper text.

Iconography: Minimal line icons with maritime motifs; filled variants for primary CTAs.

Imagery: Large nautical photos with warm overlays and subtle grain.

Surfaces: Rounded cards (12–16px radius), soft elevation, subtle sea‑glass highlights.

Core screens and required content

Home Dashboard

Hero banner for weather/alerts/promo with primary CTA (Track Nearest or View Advisory).

Active vessel count, quick shortcuts (Reorder style: Reconnect, Forecast, Route Plan).

Horizontal category chips (All, Cargo, Passenger, Fishing, Alerts).

Vertical feed of vessel cards with image/map thumbnail, name, short route, status badge, speed, ETA, primary CTA (Track) and quick Edit/Message actions.

Vessel Listing and Card Variants

Card states: Live, Docked, Delayed, Attention.

Compact list variant and expanded card (with telemetry chips: speed, heading, depth).

Inline quick actions: Track, Message, Assign.

Vessel Detail and Live Track

Hero with vessel photo or snapshot, name and operator.

Expandable live map preview with radar overlay and ETA; pinch and drag enabled.

Telemetry row: speed, heading, depth, last update.

Event log and small stepper for recent actions.

Sticky action strip: Contact, Divert, Create Task.

Task Creation Bottom Sheet

Swipeable bottom sheet with segmented controls for task type, pill toggles for priority, inputs for ETA/notes, and live validation.

Sticky CTA showing updated ETA/cost where applicable.

Dispatch Queue (Cart Analogue)

Queue of assigned tasks shown as editable cards; reorder by drag; summary totals and ETA window.

Confirm Dispatch CTA and cancel/edit options.

Tracking Timeline

Vertical stepper with timestamps and mini map thumbnails for each step (Assigned → En route → Approaching → Completed).

Contact/agent quick actions on each step and ability to attach notes/photos.

Components and interactions

Cards: 12–16px radius, subtle shadow, primary info left/top, action bottom/right.

Chips/Pills: icon + label; active/inactive styles; hit area 44px.

CTAs: Primary (coral on teal or teal on sand), Secondary (outline or muted gray).

Bottom sheet: animated elevation, swipe to dismiss, persistent sticky CTA.

Toasts: short motion, success (teal) and alerts (coral).

Motion: 120–220ms transitions with natural easing; telemetry numeric deltas animate; live badges pulse.

Accessibility and UX requirements

Color contrast: CTAs 4.5:1, secondary text 3:1.

Minimum touch target 44×44px.

Label dynamic content for screen readers (map summaries, live status).

Provide keyboard focus states and sufficient spacing for one‑hand use at sea.

Deliverables

6 annotated mobile screens (Home, Vessel Listing, Vessel Detail with expanded map, Task Modal open, Dispatch Queue, Tracking Timeline).
